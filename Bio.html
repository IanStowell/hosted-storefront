My Bio

I'm a freelance photographer based out of Indianapolis, IN and am looking to set up appointments for photoshoots! I have been shooting professionally for about 4 years now. At first it was just a hobby, but now it's a passion! You can see the photos I take for my job here.

Wether it's wedding photos, graduation photos, car photos that make any old rust bucket look brand new, or aesthetically stunning photos of abandoned buildings being ravaged by nature's beauty, I've done it all!

On the left side of the screen, you will see links to all my media accounts, and my personal gallery on Facebook.
